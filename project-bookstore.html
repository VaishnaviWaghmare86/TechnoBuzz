Full information about the 1-Book project, including business goals, features, workflow, and screenshots.

Web Application

Project Overview

This project was developed for a client who wanted an efficient digital platform to manage the daily distribution of newspapers and milk to customers. The goal of the application was to simplify the entire distribution process while maintaining accurate records of customers, delivery routes, subscriptions, and billing.

One of the core features of the system is category-based browsing and subscription management. Administrators can add different types of newspapers and milk products available for delivery. Customers can be assigned specific products according to their subscription plans. The system also allows administrators to manage special schemes or offers for regular customers.

Project Highlights

  • Responsive customer-facing catalog
  • Search, category filter, and featured titles
  • User login and order management
  • Admin dashboard for milk and NewPapers
  • Offer banners and new release showcase
